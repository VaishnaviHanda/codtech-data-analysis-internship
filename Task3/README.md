# Task 3: Interactive Sales Dashboard

## Project Title
Global Company Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Description
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze the yearly and quarterly sales performance of a company across different product categories such as Classic Cars, Motorcycles, Trucks & Buses, and Vintage Cars. The dashboard helps in understanding revenue distribution, sales trends, and key business drivers.

## Tool Used
- Microsoft Power BI

## Dataset
Company sales dataset containing order details, product categories, sales values, and time-based information.

## Dashboard Features
- Product-wise sales distribution
- Year-wise and month-wise trend analysis
- Quarterly performance comparison
- Key influencer analysis
- Interactive slicers for product selection

## Key Insights
1. Classic Cars contribute the highest revenue, accounting for nearly 54% of total sales.
2. Motorcycles are the second highest-performing category.
3. The highest sales were recorded in Q4 2004, indicating strong year-end performance.
4. Sales show significant growth after 2004, especially in late 2004 and early 2005.
5. Large deal sizes and higher order quantities have a strong positive impact on sales.
6. Vintage Cars show occasional spikes, indicating seasonal demand.

## Conclusion
This dashboard demonstrates how interactive data visualization can support business decision-making. It helps identify top-performing products, peak sales periods, and major revenue drivers, enabling management to improve sales strategies and maximize profitability.
